Instructions for Uploading Data:


  1. Log into your JumboCash account


  2. Click 'View More'


  3. Under the drop-down menu for month, select 'Last Six Months' and click 'View'


  4. Copy all of the data, beginning with the headings Date, Description, Amount, and Balance


  5. Open a new Excel file. Right click in the first cell and select 'Paste Special'


  6. Select 'Text' option


  7. Save with the extension '.csv'
