Frequently Asked Questions

Will this application help me decide what meal plan to get next semester?
It will not explicitly tell you what plan to get, but by looking at your total efficiency you can choose to adjust your meal plan and printing plan accordingly.

How do you calculate my optimal meal plan and printing plan?
We insert your JumboCash data and data from Tufts printing services and dining services into a series of algorithms to calculate how you can optimize your JumboCash.

Will you share my data with anyone?
No. We simply use your data to analyze how you use your meal plan and JumboCash. We promise not to share the data you provide us with any third parties.

Why do I need to enter my email?
We will use your email as verification if you forget your password. We will never email you spam or give your email out to any third parties
